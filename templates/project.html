"Dreamweaver" - Animated Short Film

"Dreamweaver" is a personal animated short film project that explores the journey of a young artist discovering their creative powers. This project showcases my skills in character animation, background design, and storytelling, blending 2D and 3D animation techniques to create a unique visual experience.

Key Features

  • Original story and character designs
  • Blend of 2D and 3D animation techniques
  • Custom-designed backgrounds and environments
  • Original musical score
  • Visual effects for magical elements
  • 3-minute runtime

Techniques and Tools Used

  • Character Animation: Adobe Animate, Toon Boom Harmony
  • 3D Elements: Blender
  • Compositing: Adobe After Effects
  • Background Art: Photoshop, Procreate
  • Sound Design: Adobe Audition
  • Storyboarding: Storyboard Pro

Challenges and Solutions

Challenge 1: Blending 2D and 3D Elements

Integrating 3D elements seamlessly with 2D animation while maintaining a cohesive art style was challenging.

Solution:

Developed a custom shader in Blender to render 3D elements with a 2D look, and carefully matched lighting and color palettes between 2D and 3D elements.

Challenge 2: Character Consistency

Maintaining character design consistency across different scenes and emotional states was difficult.

Solution:

Created detailed character model sheets and expression libraries. Implemented rigorous quality control checks throughout the animation process to ensure consistency.
