"Forgotten Lands" - Animated Series

Forgotten Lands is a passion project of mine that blends my love of complex worldbuilding and dynamic characters. This project is an animated series pitch that weaves together themes of friendship, magic, and self-discovery. Set in a richly imagined world brimming with mythical creatures, changelings, and faeries, the series follows two best friends, Mei and Avery, as they embark on action-packed adventures after stumbling into a realm that exists parallel to their own.
