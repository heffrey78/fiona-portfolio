About Me

Hi there! My name is Fiona Wikstrom and I'm a passionate writer, background artist, and animator currently honing my skills at the School of Visual Arts. My creative journey has led me to produce multiple films, and I thrive in collaborative environments where ideas can flow and grow.

As a fan of animated series like Adventure Time, Avatar: The Last Airbender, and Hilda, I’m drawn to the magic of storytelling and character development. I believe in the power of animation to connect people and spark imagination.

When I'm not working on my latest project, you can often find me playing D&D, baking delicious treats or exploring the woods. Nature inspires much of my art, and I love to bring that sense of wonder and wild beauty into my storytelling.

I’m excited to share my work and connect with fellow creatives. Let’s make something magical together!
